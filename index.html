About Coco Addict

A cozy coconut-themed cafe tucked along Soi Sanamklee.

Coco Addict is a friendly hidden gem in Lumphini, Bangkok, serving fresh coconut drinks, coffee, matcha, Thai tea, ice cream, pastries, and refreshing desserts. It is the kind of small local cafe that feels easy to find once and hard to forget.

Come for the coconut smoothie, stay for the chill/cozy vibe, and bring a friend for a quiet drink between the parks.
