Our Story

A cozy coconut corner in Bangkok.

Coco Addict is a small, coconut-obsessed café tucked along Soi Sanamklee in Lumphini. We serve refreshing drinks, quiet moments, and easy conversation — a little breather between the parks and the city.

Every espresso is pulled over our signature cold coconut milk, every matcha is whisked the slow way, and every scoop of coconut ice-cream comes with three toppings on the house. Simple things, done with love.
